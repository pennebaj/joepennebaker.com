Conversion Rate Optimization
If I've found a home in anything, it's CRO. I've been designing for over 10 years but never found a way to combine design with business best practices until exploring this realm.

My technique is simple: I start with experience changes deep in the funnel, usually on the product detail page, such as the Add to Cart button, images, and product description. I'll usually then work funnel-out and optimize items on category pages, then work to the home page and navigation. I always find the more I experiment, the more I learn and the more ideas I get for tests with other brands; that's what excites me the most!

In 2018, I generated over $300k in extra revenue from experimentation and learned a metric ton about a/b testing process and design.
